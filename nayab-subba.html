💡 Exam Strategy for Nayab Subba

Because Nayab Subba questions are more analytical, do not rush. Allocate roughly 10 minutes for GK, 12 minutes for Administration, 11 minutes for Math, and 9 minutes for IQ. Use the remaining 3 minutes to review flagged questions. Precision matters more than speed at this level.

Understanding the Nayab Subba Examination Structure

The Nayab Subba recruitment process conducted by Lok Sewa Aayog typically involves three stages: the preliminary examination (objective MCQs), the main examination (subjective/descriptive papers), and an interview for candidates who clear both written stages.

The preliminary exam is the great filter. It consists of 100 MCQs to be answered in 45 minutes, with negative marking of 0.25 marks per incorrect answer. The competition is intense because experienced Kharidar officers — who have already passed Lok Sewa exams once — compete alongside fresh graduates. Cutoff scores for the open category often exceed 60 marks, making every single correct answer precious.
