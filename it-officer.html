  • Software development lifecycle models: waterfall, agile, spiral, iterative

2. Database Management Systems (DBMS)

  • Relational database concepts: tables, tuples, attributes, keys (primary, foreign, candidate)
  • Entity-Relationship (ER) diagrams and database design

  • Protocols: HTTP, HTTPS, FTP, SMTP, POP3, TCP, UDP, ICMP

4. Information Security and Cybersecurity

  • Memory management: paging, segmentation, virtual memory
  • File systems: FAT, NTFS, ext4, directory structures
  • Linux commands: ls, cd, grep, chmod, chown, tar, cron, shell scripting basics

Career Prospects for IT Officer in Nepal Government

An IT Officer appointed through Lok Sewa Aayog enjoys a stable, respected career with excellent growth potential. Starting at the gazetted 3rd level, IT Officers are deployed to ministries like the Ministry of Communication and Information Technology, National Information Technology Center (NITC), and various department-level IT units. The career progression follows the standard civil service ladder with promotions to Section Officer, Under Secretary, Joint Secretary, and potentially Secretary. Many IT Officers also receive foreign training opportunities in India, Japan, South Korea, the USA, and European countries on scholarships funded by donor agencies.
